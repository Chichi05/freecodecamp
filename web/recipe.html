Chicken Soup

This is my delocious chicken recipes that contains great nutrients and great for the cold weather to keep you warm and satisfy your delicious buds...Enjoy!!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add the chicken pieces and water. Bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
  3. Add carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Simmer for another 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in egg noodles and cook for 5 more minutes until noodles are tender.
  5. Remove bay leaf and stir in parsley. Serve hot.
